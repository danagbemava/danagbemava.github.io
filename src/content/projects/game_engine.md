### Summary

Production Game Engine is an extension built on top of the Agriconnect application. It was designed to test the students understanding of the course content they had consumed during their time as part of the agriconnect project. 

### Application Model

The model of this application was to let the students make decisions which would lead different results. The results were then used to evaluate the students understanding of the course content. The decisions had weights and the results were calculated based on the weights. We also allowed students to walkthrough their decisions to understand the results.

### My Role

I was responsible for building this backend microservice. I architected a system that handles walking through the decisions and calculating the results.



### Technologies Used

- Spring Boot
- Docker
- MongoDB
