### Summary
Kaizen-Aceit is a Learning Management System (LMS) that helps students learn and manage their courses. It also allows content providers to manage their courses,assessments and other related items.

### My Role
I was responsible for the following: 

1. Kaizen-Aceit Mobile App
2. Kaizen-Aceit Content Management System

### Features 

- Course Bundle Management
- Course Management
- Assessment/FlashCard Management
- Achievement Tracking
- Content consumption tracking
- Content delivery


### Technologies Used

Mobile App: 
    Flutter , Dart, Riverpod

Backend: 
    NestJS, TypeScript, PostgreSQL, Docker, Kafka, Vimeo API
